Game Matchup Analysis
For every scheduled game, the website provides a comprehensive overview of the matchup:
● Win Probability: It compares Vegas win percentages against its own Model win
percentages for each team.
● Total Points Comparison: The platform lists the Vegas Total (over/under) and
compares it to the Model's Total projection to identify potential value.
● Situational Metrics: It includes advanced game context, such as identifying a Moderate
blowout risk (e.g., a 25% risk for the Lakers vs. Rockets game).
● Injury Reports: A detailed, up-to-date Injury Report is provided for each team, listing
players as OUT, QUESTIONABLE, or DOUBTFUL.
Player Prop Projections
The core feature of the site is its extensive list of player prop "picks" (e.g., 21 picks for the
Celtics and 26 for the 76ers). These picks include:
● Statistical Categories: Projections for Points (PTS), Rebounds (REB), Assists (AST),
Points + Rebounds + Assists (PRA), Three-pointers Made (3PM), Steals (STL), and
Blocks (BLK).
● The "Edge": The platform calculates the numerical difference between the betting line
and the model's projection to show the perceived advantage.
● Confidence Levels: Recommendations are categorized by strength, such as Strong
Lean, Lean, Marginal, or Pass.
● Matchup Environment: Each pick is assigned a label like Hostile, Tough, or
Favorable, likely indicating the difficulty of the matchup or the model's stance against
the market.
Performance Tracking & Trends
To provide context for its projections, the site tracks player performance over time:
● Rolling Form: It displays the L5 average (average stats over the last five games) and
compares this directly to the current betting line.
● Recent Trends: It highlights shifts in performance by comparing the L3 average (last
three games) to the L5 average to indicate if a player is trending up or trending down.
Market Integrity Tools
● Line Quality Alerts: The site flags markets where no recognized primary book (such as
Bovada, DraftKings, or FanDuel) has a line. It advises users to monitor these for
potential injuries, phantom markets, or late-arriving lines before placing a bet.
